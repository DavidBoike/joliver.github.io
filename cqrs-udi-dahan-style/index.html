CQRS: Udi Dahan Style

Jan 25, 2010

Mark Harris recently recorded Udi Dahan talking about CQRS when he was at a user group in Australia.

It's about 90 minutes long, but if you're trying to wrap your head around CQRS, it's a great place to start.

One other note, Udi's style is independent of "event sourcing" as a mechanism for storing and rehydrating your objects from persistence.
